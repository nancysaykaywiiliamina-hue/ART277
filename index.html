About Me

I am an incoming developer, tech innovator, and creative problem solver based in Liberia, deeply passionate about using technology to shape meaningful change. With a growing foundation in software development and a curiosity for emerging tools and trends, I am committed to exploring how digital solutions can address real-world challenges. My journey is driven by a desire to build, experiment, and continuously learn as I develop my skills and contribute to the broader tech landscape.

Beyond technical growth, I am equally inspired by the transformative power of innovation in my community. I aim to use my skills to create impactful, accessible, and user-centered technologies that improve lives and expand opportunities, especially in underserved areas. As I continue to evolve in the tech ecosystem, I hope to collaborate with forward-thinking individuals and organizations to build solutions that connect people, strengthen systems, and push the boundaries of what is possible in Liberia and beyond.

My Projects

Mobile Health Tracker App

I developed a mobile health tracker app designed to help users monitor daily habits such as hydration, exercise, and sleep. The project focuses on delivering a clean interface and personalized insights to encourage healthier lifestyles.

Community Learning Platform

I built an online platform that provides digital skills training for students and young professionals in underserved communities. The system includes interactive lessons, quizzes, and progress tracking to make learning more engaging and accessible.

Smart Agriculture Dashboard

This project uses sensor data to help farmers track soil moisture, crop health, and weather patterns in real time. It empowers local farmers by simplifying data and offering actionable recommendations to improve crop yields.

Event Management Web App

I created a web application that helps users plan, schedule, and organize events with customizable tools. The platform streamlines communication between organizers and participants, making event coordination faster and more efficient.
